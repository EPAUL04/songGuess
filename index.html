Guess My Song


Welcome! This game selects a song from your spotify library, then gives you seven attempts to guess which song has been selected. Clues are given as follows:

  1. playlist(s) you've created and added the song to
  2. genre information (via spotify)
  3. bpm and languages (if possible!!!)
  4. the artist(s)
  5. the album
  6. no extra clues!
  7. the first letter

To play, log in to your Spotify account below!
